Research

Research Interests

Identifying Unidentified PeVatron Sources

Characterizing ultra-high-energy gamma-ray sources beyond the PeV scale using multi-wavelength and multi-messenger observations with Fermi-LAT, HAWC, and IceCube.

Neutrino Astrophysics

Analyzing potential neutrino emitters from within and beyond the galaxy to constrain the hadronic fraction of the observed gamma rays.

Machine Learning for Event Reconstruction

Developing scalable reconstruction and classification algorithms for IceCube neutrino track and shower events, increasing observatory sensitivity.

Image Processing in Astrophysics

Exploring computer vision techniques for source detection and seeding algorithms applied to gamma-ray sky maps.
